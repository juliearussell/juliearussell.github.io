At some point, you're going to lose your keys

How will your keys find their way back to you? Maybe you'll remember where you lost them, or they'll get turned in somewhere that you'll decide to look. But what if you went to a pet store, made an ID tag like you would for a dog's collar, put your first name and phone number on it, and attach it to your keys?

Always pack a bathing suit

I've never regretted packing a bathing suit when traveling, even to somewhere that there's a snowball's chance I'd be wearing one. But I have spent countless hours trying to find a suitable bathing suit, out of season, or just out of fashion, when I needed one in a pinch. Plus, they take up so little room in your suitcase, why not?
