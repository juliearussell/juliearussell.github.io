A Walk in San Francisco's McLaren Park

Every day my dog Brown and I walk through McLaren Park, in the southern end of San Francisco.
It is The City's second largest park, after Golden Gate Park, but not many know about it.

From the park, there are magnificent views of downtown San Francisco, as well as a small lake,
and plenty of our city's trademark hills.

Wildlife we often see in the park:

  • ravens
  • hummingbirds
  • coyotes
